Hello!

I’m Shannon, a digital artist.

I love superheroes, fantasy and historical settings; I’m currently working on building my own portfolio with original work but I also love drawing fanarts of my favourite mangas and anime.

I’ve worked on a couple of zines and found out I enjoy managing my own projects. I enjoy collaborating with other artists and writers as well; let’s create something amazing together!

You can reach out to me through my contacts page, just shoot me an e-mail or send me a dm on social media! I speak English, Spanish and Italian so understanding eachother won’t be an issue.
